What is Stonks?

Stonks is a fun and safe way to practice investing money in the stock market. When you create an account with Stonks, you are given a fictional $1,000, which you can then invest however your heart desires. Stonks gives you the opportunity to compete against yourself and a community of users, and to hone your investing skills in a competetive yet forgiving environment. So, go ahead - make an account and get started on your investing career!
